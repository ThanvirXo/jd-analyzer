Privacy Policy — JD Match Analyzer

Last updated: May 30, 2026

JD Match Analyzer (the "Extension") is a Chrome browser extension that helps you see how well your resume matches a job description. This policy explains exactly what data the Extension handles, how it is used, and your choices. We have tried to keep this honest and plain.

Summary

  • Your resume is stored locally in your browser, not on our servers.
  • When you click Analyze, your resume text and the job description text are sent to our analysis server, which forwards them to an AI model to generate a match score.
  • We do not sell your data, show ads, or use it for advertising.
  • We do not require you to create an account.

Information we handle

  • Resume content. When you upload a PDF resume, the Extension extracts its text in your browser and saves that text in Chrome's local storage (chrome.storage.local) on your device. It stays on your device until you update or remove it.
  • Job description text. When you open the Extension on a supported job page (LinkedIn, Naukri, or Indeed), it reads the visible job description text from that page so it can be compared against your resume.

How your information is used

When you click Analyze, the Extension sends your stored resume text and the current job description text to our backend server over an encrypted (HTTPS) connection. The server passes this text to a third-party AI language model, which returns a match score, matching skills, missing skills, and a short summary. That result is displayed to you in the Extension. The text is processed to produce your result and is not used to build a profile of you.

Data storage and retention

  • On your device: your resume text remains in your browser's local storage until you choose to update or remove it, or until you uninstall the Extension.
  • On our server: the analysis request is processed in real time to generate your result. The backend does not maintain user accounts or a database of submitted resumes.

Third-party services

To generate results, your submitted text is processed by a third-party AI model provider. That processing is subject to the provider's own terms and privacy practices. We only send the resume and job description text needed to produce a match result.

Permissions and why we need them

  • Storage — to save your resume locally so you don't have to upload it every time.
  • Active tab — to read the job description from the page you are currently viewing, only when you use the Extension.
  • Host access to LinkedIn, Naukri, and Indeed — to extract the job description text from those job sites.

What we do not do

  • We do not sell or rent your personal information.
  • We do not use your data for advertising.
  • We do not track your browsing across the web.

Your choices

  • You can remove your stored resume at any time using the "Remove" option in the Extension.
  • You can stop all data processing by uninstalling the Extension.

Children's privacy

The Extension is not directed to children under 13 and we do not knowingly collect information from them.

Changes to this policy

We may update this policy from time to time. Material changes will be reflected by updating the "Last updated" date above.
